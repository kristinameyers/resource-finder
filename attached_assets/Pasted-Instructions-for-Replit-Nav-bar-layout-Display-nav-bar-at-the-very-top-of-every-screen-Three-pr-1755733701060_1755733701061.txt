Instructions for Replit:
Nav bar layout:

Display nav bar at the very top of every screen.

Three primary icons:

Menu button (hamburger) on far left. Opens a left-side slide out menu, not just one overlay.

Back button (chevron) to the right of the menu button (as shown in image, left side before logo).

211 Logo centered.

Location button (pin icon) on far right, links to “Update Location Screen.”

Ensure icons are spaced, sized, and aligned as in your mockup.

Slide out menu:

Opens from left when menu button clicks.

Buttons/links stacked vertically, each styled as a menu item.

Links to screens:

Home

About

Search by Category

Search by Keyword

Update Location

Favorites

Call 211

Settings

"Call 211" triggers tel:211 on mobile.

Summary:
Place this navbar (.global-navbar) at the top of every screen.

Use standard accessible button styles for icons.

All three icons (menu, back, location) must be easily clickable and well-spaced.

Slide out menu is full-height, left-aligned, with blue/white buttons matching your brand.

All navigation targets work as described above.
